Site Name

Bella Vista Chamber of Commerce.

I choose this name because it is the local area which Chamber of Commerce operates.

Site Purpose

Bella Vista Chamber of Commerce has the purpose of helping to grow and establish small businesses in the local area by promoting the interests of the local business community. We provide access to valuable resources, discounts, and relationships that help businesses save money and market their products.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?
